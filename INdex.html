Light

This plan includes access to the gym and its equipment during regular hours. It also includes a complimentary fitness assessment and a personalized workout plan.

Medium

This plan includes all the benefits of the Light Membership, plus unlimited access to group fitness classes, a monthly personal training session, and a discount on additional personal training sessions.

Prime

This plan includes all the benefits of the Medium Membership, plus unlimited access to the sauna and steam room, a monthly massage or spa treatment, and priority booking for personal training sessions.
