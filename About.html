Equipments that we provide

Squat Rack: This is where serious squatting takes place. In fitness and strength training, the squat exercise trains your full body. All serious strength training regiments should incorporate the squat station gym equipment.


Barbells: A barbell is essential to strength training, it holds the free weights, or sometimes the weights are attached to the ends. A barbell is perhaps the most versatile out of all the gym equipment out there. You can literally do hundreds of different exercises with these iron bars.


Hexagon Dumbbells: These have a sturdy and versatile design to develop strength and power.


Kettlebells: These are stylish weights for functional training.


Bumper Plates: These have consistent drop absorption and bounce.


Elastic Bands: These are light and versatile, with practical handles.


Jump Rope: This can improve agility and raise your heart rate.


Exercise Mat: This is a must-have for stretching and core exercises, light and portable.
